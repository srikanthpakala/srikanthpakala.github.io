Our milestones

The goal to reach the top is the reason why Greenply stands where it does today. What most see at a glance is the result of hard work and sincere efforts over a prolonged period of time. Reaching the top is no easy feat, but we are now recognized as one of India’s leading brands that offer home décor solutions.

1984

Mr. Rajesh Mittal took the first step towards the introduction of plywood industries in India by setting up a saw-mill.

1995

The equity shares of the company were listed and put up for sale.

1996

The organization was renamed as Greenply Industries Ltd.

2015

Introduction of Ask Greenply, Greenply's first consumer-facing platform.

2019

Launch of the Green Club 5 Hundred, the plywood comes with anti bacterial coating.

2020

A range of E0 plywood got introduced such as Green Club Plus Seven Hundred, Green Club 500 and Green Gold Platinum which is CARB certified as well.
