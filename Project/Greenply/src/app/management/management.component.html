Company Management

It requires smart minds and dynamic personalities to build a team with great management skills. Greenply is fortunate to have such individuals under one roof, which has given rise to an iconic brand in the plywood industry. Meet the team that is responsible for the widespread success of the brand.

Rajesh Mittal

Chairman Cum Managing Director

Mr. Rajesh Mittal, the Managing Director of Greenply Industries Limited (GIL), is popularly known as the plywood manufacturing baron of India, and there are legitimate reasons behind giving this influential personality the name. With a leadership foresight and strategic management, Rajesh Mittal has carved out an unprecedented growth path for Greenply. Since 1984, he has been working towards spreading the company’s wings in different pockets of India and abroad including Myanmar and Gabon, Africa. Today, Greenply enjoys the largest market share amongst the organized players along with the title of India’s No.1 manufacturer offering a comprehensive range of plywood, blockboard, decorative veneer, flush door, PVC board & doors as well as other specialized application products.Apart from being a man of policy and performance, Mr. Mittal is simple and grounded. With his humble nature, he addresses his peers as well as his employees in the organization – a true man of the people.

Sanidhya Mittal

Joint Managing Director

Sanidhya Mittal’s journey with Greenply Industries Limited started in the year 2012, when he decided to dedicate his time and energy to the enterprise at the very grassroots. He focused on the factory floor, learning about the manufacturing process and the technical features of products, not to mention various other quality tests which allowed him to receive a deeper understanding of the fundamentals of the business. Since 2017, he has taken the mantle of the business. His vision focuses on making Greenply a brand leader in its category, and one of the best places to work.

Manoj Tulsian

Joint Managing Director & CEO

Known for his experience across categories, Mr Manoj Tulsian is highly regarded for his out of box solutions. Honoured with the Brand Slam CFO Award in 2017, he is a regular speaker at the CFO forum while also speaking on subjects such as Infrastructure and EPC business. With his multi-faceted experience in the past on brands such as Kalpataru, VIP industries and Gabriel India Ltd, he intends to take the business to new heights.

Subir Palit

Country Head

Mr. Subir Palit, the Country Head forms the backbone of the organization. He drives cross-functional teams across sales, supply chain, finance, trade & marketing. He is responsible for the development of channel management along with customer development strategy, targeted at driving sustainable growth for the organization. His zeal to explore new domains has made him a prominent member of the organization.
